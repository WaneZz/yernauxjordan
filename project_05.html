The Calling

The Calling

MOTION DESIGN REALIZED FOR THE MOTION GRAPHICS COURSE IN MY FIRST YEAR AT IAD

I made this motion design on a Da Tweekaz remix of the music "The Calling" by TheFatRatI really wanted this project to have my own personal touch, by using a different art style that the original clip’s. I went for a style that I really appreciate: flat design. I learned a lot about After Effects during this project. I became faster, and also learned to put down my ideas more easily.

Open your eyes, watching afar, waiting to fly, this is the start.

Inspiration

INSPIRATION WHERE ARE YOU?

My inspiration came from multiple videos playing with lyrics while keeping it very simple. Like I say, it’s always better to have a simple but efficient result, than something filled with details but that’s rough and untidy.

Cinema 4D

I’ve been willing to add some 3D in my motion design since Day 1. Therefore, I used the Cinema4D After Effects module by Maxon, and created some diamonds to illustrate one of the lyric.
