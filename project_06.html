MINI-FX

DICE

FOR OUR SFX COURSE, WE HAD TO PRODUCE A SHORT FILM IN ORDER TO LEARN OF CREATE SPECIAL EFFECTS AND HOW TO INCRUST THEM IN THE SCENES WE HAD IMAGINED.

We were three students working on this movie. We had to write our scenario and draw a storyboard. We got the help of the “Image” section students who had to imagine how to shoot each one of the shots we would need to make our effects. The sound editing and dub recording was made the “Sound” section. They created the entire soundtrack.

VFX

ARROW

The arrow effect was made with After Effects and tracked with Nuke.

TRACKING 3D

I used Cinema4D to track the shot to recreate the scene in 3D. It was needed to have the sphere’s glow reflection on the walls.
